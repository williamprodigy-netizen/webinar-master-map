The ladder awareness → belief → offer ascension

Read bottom-up. Each rung: where their head is, the belief that has to land, the touchpoint that installs it.

Unaware
"This is just my life. Clock in, scroll, repeat."
Cold ad scroll-stop — a normal woman paid from her phone.
Problem aware
"I hate this, there has to be more — but I have no skill, following, or time."
Ad hook + opt-in promise: possible without those things.
Solution aware
"UGC is a real skill brands pay $300–$1,000/video for."
VSL, content-preview email, Sasha/Missy proof, the masterclass.
Product aware
"Not a scam. UGC World can teach me — $2.3M, Adobe, Perplexity."
Objection-killer email, live deal-flow reveal, Q&A by name.
Most aware
"I need to book the call and join before the cohort fills."
Webinar pitch + live-only scarcity → booked call → closer → sign.

Offer ascension

  • Free Masterclass — an evening + an email.
  • UGC Academy — $5,800 PIF or plan. Structure, 5-day sprint, Skool, support.
  • Back end — 1:1 mentorship + agency placement.

The 5 trust beliefs the webinar installs

  • The skill is real and in-demand.
  • No following, experience, or degree needed.
  • Women exactly like you did it from zero.
  • It's not a scam — here are the receipts.
  • You just have to show up and follow the path.
