5 · The ladder awareness → belief → offer ascension

Read bottom-up. Each rung = where their head is, the belief that has to land, and the touchpoint that does it. They can't buy until they've climbed every rung.

Unaware
"This is just my life. Clock in, clock out, scroll, repeat."
Cold ad scroll-stop — a normal woman getting paid from her phone.
Problem aware
"I hate this and there has to be more — but I have no skill, following, or time."
Ad hook + opt-in promise: freedom is possible without those things.
Solution aware
"UGC is a real skill brands pay $300–$1,000/video for. No following needed."
Content preview email, Sasha/Missy proof, the masterclass content itself.
Product aware
"This isn't a scam. UGC World can actually teach me this — receipts: $2.3M, Adobe, Perplexity."
Objection-killer email, the live deal-flow reveal, Q&A with their name in chat.
Most aware
"I need to book the call and join the Academy — before the cohort fills."
Webinar pitch + live-only scarcity → booked call → closer → PIF / plan.

Value / offer ascension

  • Rung 0 — Free Masterclass (the webinar). Cost to them: an evening + an email.
  • Rung 1 — UGC Academy ($5,800 PIF or payment plan). Structure, 5-day sprint, Skool community, support.
  • Rung 2 — Back end 1:1 mentorship + agency placement (brands like Perplexity sourcing trained creators).

The 5 trust beliefs the webinar must install

  • The skill is real and in-demand (brands pay now).
  • You don't need a following, experience, or a degree.
  • Women exactly like you did it from zero (Sasha, Missy, Jared).
  • It's not a scam — here are the receipts.
  • You just have to show up and follow the path.
