4 · The ladder awareness → belief → offer ascension

Read bottom-up. Each rung = where their head is, the belief that has to land, and the touchpoint that installs it.

Unaware
"This is just my life. Clock in, clock out, scroll, repeat."
Cold ad scroll-stop — a normal woman paid from her phone.
Problem aware
"I hate this and there has to be more — but I have no skill, following, or time."
Ad hook + opt-in promise: possible without those things.
Solution aware
"UGC is a real skill brands pay $300–$1,000/video for. No following needed."
VSL, content-preview email, Sasha/Missy proof, the masterclass.
Product aware
"Not a scam. UGC World can teach me — receipts: $2.3M, Adobe, Perplexity."
Objection-killer email, live deal-flow reveal, Q&A by name.
Most aware
"I need to book the call and join the Academy before the cohort fills."
Webinar pitch + live-only scarcity → booked call → closer → sign.

Offer ascension

  • Free Masterclass — cost to them: an evening + an email.
  • UGC Academy — $5,800 PIF or payment plan. Structure, 5-day sprint, Skool, support.
  • Back end — 1:1 mentorship + agency placement (Perplexity-type brand sourcing).

The 5 trust beliefs the webinar must install

  • The skill is real and in-demand.
  • No following, experience, or degree required.
  • Women exactly like you did it from zero.
  • It's not a scam — here are the receipts.
  • You just have to show up and follow the path.
